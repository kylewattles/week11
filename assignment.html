Tic-tac-toe is an ancient game that has been played by people around the world for centuries. The game's origins can be traced back to Egypt, and it has evolved through many variations and names.

Origins: The earliest evidence of tic-tac-toe is from Egypt, where 3x3 game boards were found on roofing tiles from around 1300 BCE. In the Roman Empire, a similar game called terni lapilli was played around the first century BC. In ancient India, a version called Pada was played using shells or stones.
